The recent friendlies are a softer note worth flagging. Even so, the group looks comfortable: a 74.7% chance of winning it and a 99.2% likelihood of clearing the first knockout round. The strength here is depth and balance rather than any single name — three credible goalkeepers in Joan García, David Raya and Unai Simón, and a defence built around Pau Cubarsí, Cucurella, Laporte and Grimaldo. The numbers say contender; the friendlies say prove it.
