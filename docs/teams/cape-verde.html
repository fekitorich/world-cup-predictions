It gives them a 31% chance of surviving the group into the round of 32, but only 4.3% to reach the last 16 and 1.4% to actually win their group. Beyond that the numbers thin out fast — under 1% to reach the quarter-finals. Realistically this is a tournament about competing hard and seeing whether that defensive solidity travels.
