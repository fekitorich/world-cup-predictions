Their route through CONCACAF qualifying was the story: unbeaten across the run shown here, with home wins over Jamaica (2-0) and goalless draws away to Trinidad and Tobago and Jamaica that did the quiet work. The model treats this realistically: just an 8.1% chance of reaching the round of 32 and 0.5% of winning the group, with the knockout numbers thinning to almost nothing beyond. Qualifying was the achievement here; everything in the tournament itself would be genuine overperformance against the odds.
