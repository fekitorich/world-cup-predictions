That cautious read flows from Ecuador's defensive record: a side that drew 0-0 with Canada and Paraguay and held the Netherlands and Morocco to 1-1 isn't easy to break down, and the model expects a grind. Germany are favoured at 38.9% to Ecuador's 29.2%, but with the draw at 31.8% this is closer to a genuine three-way toss-up than the ranking gap suggests.

That's where the model and the market part ways sharply. The two sides also diverge on goals: the model leans under, pricing over 2.5 at just 32.8% and both teams to score at 40.7%, where the market sees a livelier game at 49.5% and 52.5%. Take your pick; the model says tight and open.
